Features
  • Display optimized for desktop browsers, as well as for tablet and smartphone by using Bootleaf, Bootstrap 3 and Leaflet.
  • Multilingualism with the help of i18next. Possibility to switch between languages.
  • Presentation of the route against the background of an OpenStreetMap Karte map
  • Follow the route with the help of Leaflet locateControl
  • Representation of the sights along the route.
  • List of sights in the sidebar for the display on the desktop.
  • Modal dialogue for the representation of each attraction with text and image.
  • Modal dialogue to show the distances between the sights and estimated duration when the route is covered on foot without a break.
  • Marker clustering at higher zoom levels to increase display speed with Leaflet marker cluster plugin.
  • Download prepared information in cvs and gpx format.
