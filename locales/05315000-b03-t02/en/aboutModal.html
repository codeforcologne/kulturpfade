About the Project

  • Cologne's Kulturpfade (cultural paths) were first developed towards the end of the 1970's. They were intended to raise awareness of monument protection in post-war Cologne. The paths were marked by markers installed on buildings along the route. These markers described the building and settlement history of the respective building and provided an overview of neighboring points on the path.

    The markers were no longer locatable, lost or destroyed. Some were renewed and renovated by local district associations in the 2010s. Although there were some publications on the cultural paths, knowledge about them is now only fragmentary.

    This project of the OK Lab Cologne in collaboration with the city of Cologne aims to reserve the surving sources and make them accessible by developing a Kulturpfade application. The application allows users to explore the cultural paths and enable an option to update the information with additional data.
