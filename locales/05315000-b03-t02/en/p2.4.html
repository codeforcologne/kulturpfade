The desilting facility located in the Äußeren Grüngürtel near Berrenrather Straße was used to purify the fresh water before it was directed into Cologne. It was part of the older Vorgebirgsleitung, constructed in the middle of the 1st century A.D., which sourced its water from the Hürth area.
